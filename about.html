Company Story


Prakash Saboo & CO. is a Chartered Accountancy firm established in the year 1996. The firm has its head office based in Mumbai with a branch in Jaipur. In the year 2019 after merger of M/s AVK Sharma & Company, Our company’s strength is increased in another era of business.

The firm is backed by a strong team of professionals with expertise in handling assignments of medium to very large organizations. The firm has undertaken statutory & internal audits of various government & insurance companies, private & public limited companies, NGO's and other social organizations. The firm has experience in providing wide range of services to the clients in diversified areas such as industrial manufacturing, electronics, publications, garments, real-estate & construction, medical services, education, agro based industries, tourism, information technology and telecommunications. The firm has specialized experience in corporate & project finance, Revival, Restructuring & turn around resolution & Business advisory consultancy.
