Our Services

What We Do

Audit Assignments

The firm has carrying out various types of Audit e.g. statutory audit, Internal Audit, Revenue Audit and Stock audit of private sector undertakings Including Corporate Clients.

Taxation Services

We are dealing in direct and indirect taxation matters of corporate, individuals, partnership firms, LLP, HUF etc. We are handling income tax planning of the corporate and non corporate clients. We also represent our clients at Income tax and GST department at the assessment and appeal level.

Corporate Advice and Collaboration Agreements

In respect of company formations, we are providing comprehensive advices and ensure that the many and varied procedures for company formations are complied within a timely basis. We assist in the day to day compliances on Company Law matters such as registration of charge, preparation of annual returns, Directors report, ,filing of various returns, documents with registrar of Companies, preparation and drafting of minute books etc

Financial Services

We are arranging fund based (Working Capital/TL) and non fund based (BG/LC) financial assistance from the banks to our clients. We also help our clients in preparation of CMA data and improvement of their financial indicators. We have successfully handled various proposals of up to Rs. 25 Crore.
